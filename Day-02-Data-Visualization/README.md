# Day 2 - Data Visualization

## 🎯 Objective
Learn how to visualize data using Matplotlib and Seaborn to better understand patterns, distributions, and relationships in the Student Performance dataset.

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

- Histogram
- Count Plot
- Box Plot
- Scatter Plot
- Correlation Heatmap
- Bar Plot

---

## 🛠 Libraries Used

- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

---

## 📊 Visualizations Created

1. Distribution of Final Grades (Histogram)
2. Male vs Female Students (Count Plot)
3. Study Time Distribution (Count Plot)
4. Final Grades (Box Plot)
5. Absences vs Final Grades (Scatter Plot)
6. Correlation Heatmap

---

## 📌 Key Observations

- Most students scored in the average range.
- The dataset has a balanced distribution of male and female students.
- Most students belong to the lower study-time categories.
- A few outliers are present in the final grades.
- Students with fewer absences generally tend to perform better.
- Previous grades (G1 and G2) have the strongest correlation with the final grade (G3).
- Study time appears to have a positive relationship with academic performance.
